Meet the baker

Hi, I'm Tina!

I'm a Vietnamese American baker based in San Jose, CA. What started as a pandemic hobby quickly turned into something I truly love. Every cake is handcrafted by me with carefully chosen flavors inspired by my Asian heritage.

The name "The Little Baker" is a nod to both the tiny bento cakes I make and, well, my height (4'10" and proud!). Each 4-inch cake is made to order with fresh ingredients and lots of love.
