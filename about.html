Meet the baker

Getting a cake for someone's birthday is a love language — a testament to who truly knows them.

At The Little Baker, I create beautiful, one-of-a-kind cakes designed to spark joy and exceed expectations. Cakes are part of people's happiest moments, and I'm honored to be part of those experiences — whether it's a small celebration or something bigger.

My story

From a Bay Area kitchen
to a viral little bakery

I was born and raised in San Jose, California, and I'm a proud Vietnamese daughter of an immigrant family. Although I've always loved baking, I never imagined it could become a career — especially growing up in the Bay Area, where tech felt like the only path.

During COVID, I started sharing my cakes online, and what began as a passion project unexpectedly went viral. I'm entirely self-taught, learning through years of trial and error — and I wouldn't change a single messy batch of it.
