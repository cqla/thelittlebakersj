Good to know

Everything you might be wondering before placing an order.

We release a curated menu of preset designs and flavors once a month. Designs and flavors are fixed (no substitutions), quantities are limited, and orders are pickup only. Follow us on Instagram to be notified when the menu drops.

Submit a Custom Cake & Event inquiry. Custom cakes start at $50 and require a minimum of 2 weeks notice. Availability is limited and not guaranteed — we'll review your request and follow up with pricing and next steps.

Yes! We create whimsical one-tier wedding cakes and curated dessert tables. Visit our Weddings page for packages and minimums. Bookings are by inquiry only and require a 50% non-refundable deposit to secure your date.

Light chiffon cakes with stabilized whipped cream frosting. Asian-inspired flavors like ube, Thai tea, and matcha alongside comforting classics like vanilla, chocolate, and mocha — always not too sweet, but full of flavor.

We're based in San Jose, CA. Pickup is typically between 3–5 PM. Delivery is available for events and weddings for an additional fee.

Venmo and Zelle. For weddings and large events, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to book, with the balance due before your date.

Wheat, dairy, and eggs. No nuts! Please let us know about any allergies when you order.

Keep your cake refrigerated in its original packaging. It's best enjoyed within 5 days.

We don't ship — the cakes are too delicate to travel. We offer pickup in San Jose, plus delivery for events and weddings.

For preorders, order when the monthly menu drops (they sell quickly!). For custom cakes, allow at least 2 weeks. For weddings and larger events, please inquire as early as possible.
